Item features in resident evil 4

Storage system

With the storage system you will be able to manage your items as well as create potions. to help you in battle

Weapon upgrade

Every weapon you obtain can be upgraded for more fire power against enemies, faster reload, you name it.

Trade system

With the trade system you decide if you want the money at the moment or wait and trade it for some special items in the future.

Jewellery

With the jewellry system in re 4 you will be capable of insert gems to crowns and other items to increase its value
